About

I diagnose and treat cancer in companion animals and provide rapid, precise cytology for general practices and referral clinics. The focus is evidence-based care, clear communication, and practical treatment plans that respect quality of life.
Clinical focus
  • Medical oncology for dogs and cats
  • Diagnostic cytology and sample triage
  • Tumor staging, treatment planning, follow-up
  • Owner counseling and second opinions
